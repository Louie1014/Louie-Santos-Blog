How I Balance Gaming and School

By Louie James Santos

Gaming is super fun, but I also need to focus on school. Here's what I do:

  • I finish my homework first before I start gaming.
  • I play games as a reward after studying.
  • I make sure not to stay up too late so I can wake up early for school.
